PG

PGs usually offer better security & amenities when compared to hostels. PGs are way less community-oriented than hostels and offer almost no scope for networking. Food in a hostel mess is a thing of nightmares whereas PGs usually provide simple but tasty meals.

PG accommodations have emerged as an exceedingly lucrative option for students as they often provide better facilities of stay and food, leisure areas, large space as compared to hostels, besides offering a sense of independence that most students long for.

PG

Comfortable Rooms

We realised that the safety of an individual is far more in accommodations like a PG/ Hostel due to the very fact that you live among people mostly of your age and at any given point of time there would always be someone around to help in case of emergency.

Most of the PGs offer three meals a day, along with spacious rooms and essential furniture, common entertainment and leisure areas and even have maintenance staff to attend to the daily chores. Moreover, you could have these features customised to your needs.
